EXAMIFY FAQ

Test Generator
Q: How does the Test Generator feature work?
A: Users can upload a PDF/Image/Docx file and receive an online mock assessment (text only) with the option to upload an image for each question.
Q: Can I include images in my questions?
A: Yes, you have the option to upload images along with your questions in the PDF/Image/Docx files.

Answer Key
Q: What is the purpose of the Answer Key feature?
A: Users can upload the PDF/Image/Docx of the answer key along with the question paper to automatically correct their attempts.
Q: How does the system correct my answers?
A: The system matches your responses with the uploaded answer key to provide automatic corrections.

Timer, Tools, and Reviews
Q: Is there a timer for the mock exams?
A: Yes, there is a timer feature to track your remaining time while attempting the mock.
Q: Can I review and edit my test before finalizing it?
A: Yes, you can make a manual online assessment (text and image) without needing a PDF and then review and finalize the test before making it public.

Social Space
Q: What is the Social Space feature?
A: Users can make their tests public, allowing different users to attempt and discuss them.
Q: Can I see others' tests and discussions?
A: Yes, you can view and participate in discussions on public tests created by other users.

Analytical Side
Q: What kind of analysis does the system provide?
A: The system provides detailed analysis, including time spent on each question and subject, time wasted on questions, total score, negatives, and comparisons with peers.
Q: How can I use the analytical information?
A: You can use the analysis to identify areas for improvement, understand your performance trends, and compare your scores with peers.
